Amrita public school

education for living

It is a Senior Secondary School affiliated to CBSE New Delhi whose foundation was laid by a great visionary Mr. Joginder Singh Chhillar. The school was established in 1987 and is now one of the premiere institutions of Delhi. A team of dedicated staff comprising both teaching and non-teaching staff takes care of more than 2000 students. The quality education is delivered and young minds are given shape accordingly. It is a progressive, English-medium, co-educational school having futuristic infrastructure, spacious well ventilated classrooms, smartclasses equipped with computer aided learning round the clock, internet access, well equipped labs, activity rooms, well stocked library, auditorium and multipurpose hall. This is an institution which is known for teaching and quality education, moral values, discipline, responsiblity and respect for culture.
